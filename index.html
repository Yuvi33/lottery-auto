Frequently Asked Questions

What time does Lottery Sambad result come today?
Nagaland State Lottery Sambad results are declared 3 times daily. The 1 PM draw is usually published by 1:15 PM, the 6 PM draw by 6:15 PM, and the 8 PM draw by 8:15 PM.
How do I claim my Nagaland Lottery prize?
If you win an amount above ₹10,000, you must surrender the original ticket, a properly filled claim application, and valid government ID proof (Aadhaar/PAN) to the Directorate of Nagaland State Lotteries, or any designated bank, within 30 days of the draw.
Is it legal to play Nagaland State Lottery online?
Yes, Nagaland State Lotteries is 100% legal. It is regulated by the Government of Nagaland under the Lotteries (Regulation) Act, 1998. However, tickets should only be purchased from authorized physical dealers. Buying tickets through unauthorized third-party apps is done at your own risk.
